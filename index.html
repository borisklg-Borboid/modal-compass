About Modal Compass Pro

Modal Compass Pro is a high-end interactive educational platform designed specifically for music teachers, students, and theoretical enthusiasts. It serves as a visual bridge between abstract music theory and practical application.

The Purpose of this Tool

Understanding the relationship between church modes, scale degrees, and diatonic harmony can be challenging. This tool provides an instant, interactive visualization of these concepts across all 12 keys. It is optimized for use on smartboards in classrooms, tablets in study environments, and desktops for composers.

Key Features

  • Interactive Root Selection: Drag the outer ring to change the key. All intervals and harmonic relations update instantly.
  • Modal Analysis: Select any mode (Ionian to Locrian) to visualize its unique interval structure and characteristic notes.
  • Harmonic Visualization: Explore the chords built on each scale degree. Highlighting a chord shows its notation on the musical staff, reinforcing the connection between theory and sight-reading.
  • Professional Study Material: The high-quality PNG export feature allows educators to create custom lesson sheets and students to save their harmonic findings.

How to use

1. Rotate the outer ring to find your desired key.
2. Click on a scale degree (Roman Numerals I-VII) to choose a mode.
3. Select a chord label in the harmony panel to highlight its notes on the staff.
4. Toggle between Dark and Light mode for optimal visibility in any environment.

Created to empower the next generation of musicians with deep theoretical insights.
