About Modal Compass Pro

Modal Compass Pro is a high-end interactive educational platform designed specifically for music teachers, students, and theoretical enthusiasts. It serves as a visual bridge between abstract music theory and practical application.

The Purpose of this Tool

Understanding the relationship between church modes, scale degrees, and diatonic harmony is a cornerstone of musical mastery. This tool provides an instant, interactive visualization of these concepts across all 12 keys. It is optimized for use on smartboards in classrooms, tablets in study environments, and desktops for composers.

Key Features

  • Interactive Root Selection: Drag the outer ring to select your root note. All intervals and harmonic relations update instantly.
  • Modal Analysis: Select any of the seven church modes (Ionian to Locrian) to visualize its unique interval structure and characteristic notes.
  • Diatonic Harmony: Explore the chords built on each scale degree. Highlighting a chord shows its notation on the musical staff, reinforcing the connection between theory and sight-reading.
  • Professional Study Material: Use the PNG export feature to create custom lesson sheets for students or save your harmonic findings for personal reference.

How to use

1. Rotate the outer ring to set your desired key.
2. Click on a scale degree (Roman Numerals I-VII) to activate a specific mode.
3. Select a chord label in the harmony panel to visualize the chord structure on the staff.
4. Toggle between Dark and Light mode for optimal visibility in any teaching or learning environment.

Created to empower musicians with deep, visual theoretical insights.
